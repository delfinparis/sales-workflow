## Common Situations

### "I can't find them on Courted"
Skip them. Move to the next lead. Don't send a generic message.

### "They're on Courted but no recent superlative"
Skip them for now. They might get one later.

### "Do Not Contact is checked"
**NEVER** text them. Skip immediately.

### "Status isn't New Lead"
They're in someone else's workflow. Skip.

### "I already texted them before"
Check "Last Superlative Date" - if it's recent, skip. If it's months old AND they have a NEW superlative, you can text again.

### "They replied to me"
Great! Change status to "Replied - Awaiting Ana" and let Ana know. Don't continue the conversation yourself.
