## During the Meeting

### Opening (5 min)
```
"Hey [Name]! Great to finally connect. Ana's told me great things
about you. Before I dive in, I'd love to hear - what made you
interested in chatting today?"

Listen carefully. Take notes.
```

### Discovery (15-20 min)
```
KEY QUESTIONS:
‚Ä¢ "Tell me about your current situation at [Brokerage]"
‚Ä¢ "What's working well for you there?"
‚Ä¢ "What would you change if you could?"
‚Ä¢ "Where do you want to be in 2-3 years?"
‚Ä¢ "What would your ideal brokerage look like?"

LISTEN FOR:
‚ö†Ô∏è Specific frustrations (capture these!)
‚ö†Ô∏è Goals they're not achieving
‚ö†Ô∏è Support they're not getting
‚ö†Ô∏è Money they're leaving on the table
```

### Present Value (10-15 min)
```
Match Kale's offerings to THEIR specific needs:

If they want better splits:
‚Üí "At Kale, our structure is [X]. Based on your volume, that means..."

If they want more support:
‚Üí "We have [specific program]. For example, [story of agent who benefited]..."

If they want better tech:
‚Üí "Let me show you what we use for [their pain point]..."

If they want growth:
‚Üí "Here's how we help agents grow their business..."
```

### Handle Objections
```
Common objections and responses:

"I need to think about it"
‚Üí "Totally understand. What specifically would you want to think through?
   Maybe I can help clarify."

"I'm talking to other brokerages"
‚Üí "Smart to explore your options. What are you liking about them?
   Let's make sure you're comparing apples to apples."

"The timing isn't right"
‚Üí "When would be better timing? What would need to change?"
   (Set follow-up for that time)

"I'm worried about leaving my clients"
‚Üí "Your clients are yours. Here's how the transition works..."

"What if it doesn't work out?"
‚Üí "Here's our track record with agents like you... [success stories]"
```

### Close or Next Step (5 min)
```
IF THEY'RE READY:
"Based on everything we discussed, I think you'd be a great fit.
I'd love to put together an offer for you. Can I send that over today?"

IF THEY NEED TIME:
"Totally get it. What would be helpful for you to make a decision?
Can we schedule a follow-up call for [specific date]?"

IF THEY'RE NOT READY:
"No pressure at all. Let's stay in touch - I'll check in with you
in [timeframe]. Does that work?"
```

## Quick Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Lead went cold after hot meeting | Check in - life happens. Offer flexibility. |
| They keep rescheduling | "I want to respect both our time. Is this still something you're interested in?" |
| They're comparing to competitor | "What would help you make the best decision for yourself?" |
| They want things we can't offer | Be honest. Better to lose now than have unhappy agent later. |
| They signed with competitor | "Congrats! If things don't work out, door is always open." Set win-back date. |

## Remember

- **Preparation wins** - Know their story before the call
- **Listen more than you talk** - They should do 60% of talking
- **Match value to needs** - Don't pitch features, solve problems
- **Follow up consistently** - Most deals close on follow-up 3-7
- **Know when to let go** - Not every lead is a fit, and that's okay
- **Celebrate wins** - And share credit with Ana and Jennica!

**Jennica plants seeds. Ana waters them. You harvest.**
