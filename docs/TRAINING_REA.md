# Rea's Daily Training Guide

## Your Role in 30 Seconds

You handle **newly licensed brokers** - people who just passed their real estate exam. They get imported from a monthly list, receive automated texts from Close CRM, and when they reply, YOU take over. Your job is to get them scheduled with DJ.

**Your goal:** Turn replies into appointments. Be fast, be helpful, be persistent.

---

## Getting Started (One-Time Setup)

### Tools You Need Open
1. **Monday.com** - "Newly Licensed Leads" board
2. **Close CRM** - Where automated texts come from (you may need to check replies here)
3. **JustCall** - For manual SMS/calls
4. **Calendly** - DJ's meeting link

### Your Monday Board
Your board is **"Newly Licensed Leads"** with these groups:
- **Never Responded** - New imports, automated sequence running
- **Responded** - They replied! Your priority.
- **Scheduled Appointments** - Booked with DJ
- **Initially Responded, Then Disappeared** - Engaged once, then ghosted

## Working New Replies (Priority 1)

When someone shows up in the "Responded" group:

### Step 1: Read What They Said
```
1. Click on the lead in Monday
2. Check Close CRM or JustCall for their message
3. Note their tone - excited? skeptical? just curious?
```

### Step 2: Respond With Calendly Link
```
Your goal: Get them scheduled with DJ.

RESPONSE TEMPLATE:
---
"Hey [Name]! Thanks for getting back to me!

Would love to set up a quick call with our broker DJ to answer any questions
about getting started. Here's his calendar - grab any time that works:

[Calendly Link]

What questions do you have so far?"
---
```

### Step 3: Update Monday
```
‚ñ° Status should already be "Replied" (automation)
‚ñ° If not, change it to "Replied"
‚ñ° Make sure they're in "Responded" group
‚ñ° Set "Next Action": "Wait for booking or follow up"
‚ñ° Set "Next Action Date": Tomorrow
```

## The Call Follow-Up Sequence (Priority 2)

**If they don't book within 24 hours after your Calendly text:**

Start calling. Every day for 7 days.

### Day 1-7 Call Script
```
WHEN THEY ANSWER:
"Hey [Name]! This is Rea from Kale Realty. I sent you a text
about setting up a call with our broker DJ. Do you have a quick
minute to find a time?"

‚Üí If yes: Help them book via Calendly or pick a time verbally
‚Üí If busy: "No problem! When's a better time to chat?"
‚Üí If not interested: "Totally understand. What are you looking for
   in a brokerage?" (listen, then offer DJ call again)

WHEN YOU GET VOICEMAIL:
"Hey [Name], this is Rea from Kale Realty. Just following up on
my text about connecting with our broker DJ. Give me a call back
at [your number] or just reply to my text with a good time. Talk soon!"
```

### Tracking Call Attempts
```
After each call:
‚ñ° Update "Last Contact Date"
‚ñ° Add Note: "Call #1 - voicemail" or "Call #3 - spoke, will call back"
‚ñ° Set "Next Action Date" for tomorrow
```

### After 7 Days of Calls With No Response
```
‚ñ° Status ‚Üí "Lost - No Response"
‚ñ° Add Note: "7 call attempts, no response"
‚ñ° They get added to event invite list for future
```

## Special Situations

### They Already Joined Another Firm
```
"I already joined [Brokerage]"

1. Respond nicely: "Congrats! Hope it's going well for you!"
2. Status ‚Üí "Chose Another Firm"
3. Set "Hibernation Until" ‚Üí Today + 90 days
4. In 90 days: They'll come back for a check-in
```

### They Say "Stop" or "Leave Me Alone"
```
1. Respond: "No problem, removing you from our list. Good luck!"
2. Status ‚Üí "Do Not Contact"
3. Check the "Do Not Contact" checkbox
4. NEVER contact them again
```

### They Responded Once, Then Disappeared
```
After 3+ days with no reply after booking attempt:
1. Move to "Initially Responded, Then Disappeared" group
2. Lower priority than active "Responded" leads
3. Try again in 1-2 weeks with a fresh approach
```
