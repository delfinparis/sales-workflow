## The Relationship Flow

Here's how conversations typically progress:

### Phase 1: Initial Engagement
```

- "What's your biggest challenge right now?"
- "What would make your business easier?"
```

## Handling Special Situations

### They Stop Responding (Stale Lead)
```
After 7 days with no reply:
1. Send a gentle follow-up:
   "Hey [Name]! Just checking in - hope all is well!
    Anything I can help with?"
2. Wait another 7 days
3. If still no reply after 14 days total:
   - Status ‚Üí "30-Day Timeout"
   - They'll come back to Jennica eventually
```

### They Say "Stop" or Get Hostile
```
1. Respond politely: "No problem at all. Take care!"
2. Status ‚Üí "Dead - Negative Reply"
3. Done. Never contact again.
```

### They Want to Talk to Someone Now
```
1. Get them to DJ quickly
2. Send Calendly link
3. Status ‚Üí "Meeting Scheduled"
4. Fill in Meeting Date when they book
```

### They Mention a Competitor
```
"I'm actually talking to [Other Brokerage]"
1. Don't panic
2. "Oh nice! What's drawing you to them?"
3. Listen. Take notes.
4. "Makes sense. Well, if you want to compare options, happy to connect
    you with DJ for a no-pressure chat."

UPDATE COMPETITIVE INTEL:
‚ñ° Competitor Name - Select from dropdown (@properties, Compass, etc.)
‚ñ° Competitor Reason - Why they're attracted to that brokerage
```

### No Show to Meeting with DJ
```
When DJ marks "No Show":
1. Lead comes back to you
2. Send: "Hey [Name]! Looks like we missed you - everything okay?
   Happy to reschedule whenever works for you."
3. Send new Calendly link
4. If they reschedule ‚Üí back to DJ
5. If they ghost again ‚Üí Status ‚Üí "Hibernation 90-Day"
```
