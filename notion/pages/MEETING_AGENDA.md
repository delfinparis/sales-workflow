## Meeting Flow (30-45 min)

### 1. Opening (5 min)

**Goal:** Build rapport, set the tone

```
"Hey [Name]! Great to finally connect. Ana's told me great
things about you. Before I dive into anything, I'd love to
hear from you - what made you interested in chatting today?"
```

- [ ] Let them talk first
- [ ] Take notes on what they say
- [ ] Acknowledge what Ana shared

---

### 2. Discovery (15-20 min)

**Goal:** Understand their situation deeply

**Questions to ask:**

**Current Situation:**
- "Tell me about your business right now"
- "How long have you been at [Current Brokerage]?"
- "What's working well for you there?"

**Pain Points:**
- "What would you change if you could?"
- "What's the biggest challenge you're facing right now?"
- "What's frustrating you about [thing they mentioned]?"

**Goals:**
- "Where do you want to be in 2-3 years?"
- "What does your ideal brokerage look like?"
- "What would success look like for you?"

### 3. Present Value (10-15 min)

**Goal:** Match Kale's offerings to THEIR needs

**Only talk about what's relevant to them:**

| If they want... | Show them... |
|-----------------|--------------|
| Better splits | Our compensation structure + math |
| More support | Training programs, mentorship |
| Better tech | Platform demo, tools |
| Growth help | Business development resources |
| Better culture | Team stories, values |
| Leads | Lead gen programs |

**Use stories:**
> "I had an agent who was in a similar situation... [relevant success story]"

---

### 4. Handle Objections (5-10 min)

**Goal:** Address concerns directly

- [ ] Ask: "What questions do you have?"
- [ ] Ask: "What concerns come to mind?"
- [ ] Don't rush past objections
- [ ] Use Objection Playbook responses

---

### 5. Close or Next Step (5 min)

**Goal:** Clear outcome

**If they're ready:**
> "Based on everything we discussed, I think you'd be a great fit. I'd love to put together an offer for you. Can I send that over today?"

**If they need time:**
> "Totally get it. What would be helpful for you to make a decision? Can we schedule a follow-up call for [specific date]?"

**If they need to talk to someone:**
> "Of course. Would it help if I joined a call with you and [spouse/partner]?"

**If they're not ready:**
> "No pressure at all. Let's stay in touch - I'll check in with you in [timeframe]. Does that work?"
