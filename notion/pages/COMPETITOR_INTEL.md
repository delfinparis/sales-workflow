## How to Use This Page

1. **Never trash competitors** - Stay professional
2. **Focus on differences** - Not "better," just "different"
3. **Know their strengths** - Acknowledge what they do well
4. **Know their gaps** - Where Kale fills the void

## When They Mention a Competitor

**Step 1:** Don't panic!

**Step 2:** Ask questions
> "What's drawing you to them?"

**Step 3:** Listen and take notes

**Step 4:** Acknowledge strengths
> "Yeah, they do [X] well."

**Step 5:** Differentiate (don't attack)
> "The main difference with us is [specific value]."

**Step 6:** Offer comparison
> "Want me to put together a side-by-side so you can compare?"
